Super Store Sales Performance & Forecast Dashboard (Power BI)

⸻

📌 Project Summary

The Super Store Sales Dashboard is a data-driven analytics solution developed using Power BI to monitor and evaluate retail sales performance across different regions, categories, and customer segments.

This dashboard provides interactive insights into sales, profit, product categories, shipping modes, payment methods, and regional performance. It also includes sales forecasting for the next 15 days, helping businesses understand trends and make data-driven decisions.

⸻

🎯 Project Objectives
	•	Analyze overall sales and profit performance
	•	Identify top-performing product categories and sub-categories
	•	Compare sales performance across regions and states
	•	Evaluate customer segments and payment modes
	•	Provide sales forecasting insights for short-term planning

⸻

🛠 Tools & Technologies
	•	Power BI Desktop – Dashboard Development
	•	DAX (Data Analysis Expressions) – KPI calculations
	•	Power Query – Data cleaning and transformation
	•	Data Modeling – Table relationships and optimization

⸻

📈 Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

The dashboard tracks major business metrics including:
	•	Total Sales: 2M
	•	Total Profit: 175K
	•	Total Quantity Sold: 22K
	•	Average Delivery Time: 10 Days

These KPIs provide a quick overview of the store’s overall performance.

⸻

📊 Dashboard Insights & Visual Analysis

🔹 Sales by Segment
	•	Consumer segment contributes the highest share of total sales
	•	Corporate and Home Office segments follow respectively

🔹 Payment Mode Analysis
	•	Cash on Delivery (COD) accounts for the largest portion of transactions
	•	Online and card payments contribute significantly

🔹 Regional Performance
	•	West and East regions generate the highest sales
	•	South region shows comparatively lower sales performance

🔹 Category & Sub-Category Analysis
	•	Office Supplies generate the highest sales
	•	Technology and Furniture follow closely
	•	Phones, Chairs, and Binders are among the top-selling sub-categories

🔹 Shipping Mode Insights
	•	Standard Class shipping is the most frequently used delivery method
	•	Same Day shipping is the least used option

🔹 State-Level Sales

Top-performing states include:
	•	California
	•	New York
	•	Texas
	•	Washington

🔹 Sales Trend & Forecast
	•	Monthly sales trends analyzed for 2019 and 2020
	•	15-day sales forecasting added using Power BI analytics features

⸻

🎛 Interactive Features
	•	Region filter buttons (Central, East, South, West)
	•	Drill-down enabled charts for deeper insights
	•	Interactive category and sales trend analysis
	•	Forecast visualization for upcoming sales trends
